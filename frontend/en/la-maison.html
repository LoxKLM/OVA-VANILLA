Ova Vanilla is a French trading house specialising in direct import of Gourmet vanilla from cooperatives in the Sava region of Madagascar.

Our story

From grower
to professional.

Ova Vanilla was born from a simple observation: the vanilla supply chain is far too long. Between the Malagasy grower and the French pastry chef, there are sometimes four or five intermediaries, each taking a margin that erodes final quality and fair remuneration for the producer.

We chose the direct approach. Partnerships with cooperatives in Sambava, quality control carried out on site before shipment, and a permanent stock enabling delivery within five working days.

The name Ova — meaning "egg" in Malagasy — evokes the pod before flowering, the promise of an aroma still locked inside.

Our commitments

What sets
us apart.

Direct import

No intermediary between the Malagasy cooperative and our warehouse. Quality is preserved; the price is fair for the producer.

Full traceability

Every lot carries a reference number (e.g. SV-254) linked to a harvest, a geographic zone and a precise quality control date.

Permanent stock

Unlike many trading houses bound by seasonality, we maintain a permanent stock. Delivery within 5 working days across France and internationally.

B2B responsiveness

Quotes within 24 hours. Orders from 500 g. Volume-based pricing. Dedicated contact for regular accounts.

International delivery

We ship to France, Europe, the United States and Canada. Professional packaging, lot-by-lot traceability, quality certificate included with every shipment.

Long-term partnership

Our relationships with Sava cooperatives are built on multi-year contracts. Security for the producer, consistency for our professional clients.
