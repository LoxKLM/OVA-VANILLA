The House

A trading house,
pod by pod.

Ova Vanilla supplies bakers, pastry chefs and restaurateurs with pods and extracts selected directly from Sava cooperatives. Our role: identify the best batches, inspect them on receipt, and deliver them to professionals who demand reliable, fully documented ingredients.

Direct sourcing

Direct relationships with Sava cooperatives. No broker, no untraced batch.

Inspection on receipt

Every batch inspected on arrival: moisture level, size, organoleptic. Quality certificate included with each delivery.

B2B delivery

Professional pack sizes, national and international shipping. Custom pricing based on volume and regularity.
